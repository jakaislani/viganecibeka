    G Herbo and Taina Williams are enjoying summer with their kids. The celebrity parents shared pictures from the family’s recent outing at the pool in Miami Beach, Florida.

    Taina uploaded pictures of her son, Essex, and daughter, Emmy Love, enjoying a day of fun. Emmy wore sunglasses while relaxing alongside her family. “Emmy looks like mommy and daddy,” one social media follower said of Taina and G Herbo’s daughter. “Look at lil mama,” another fan commented.

    Essex was also photographed with his mom and dad. The youngster took several pictures while in the pool with his mom. Taina held her son gently while he explored the water. “Oh my goodness,” one social media follower said in reference to all the cuteness. “I can’t,” another fan exclaimed. “Cuteness overload.”

    Essex made his mom a parent for the first time last year. Taina commemorated Mother’s Day as a mom for the first time this year. “I promise to give you everything that I have,” Williams wrote to her son. “There’s not a dream in this world you can’t accomplish or grasp,” Taina told Essex.

    All three of G Herbo’s kids make him a doting dad. The celebrity father recently shared how becoming a father for the first time to his oldest son, Yosohn, changed his life in the best way possible.

    “He’s always in the studio with me,” G Herbo told the media. “I have a son,” the recording artist continued. “I have a life that’s depending on me. Hearing his voice on there around the stuff that I’m talking about, it gives you that surrealness.”

    Yosohn is G Herbo’s son with social media influencer Ari Fletcher. He celebrated his fourth birthday earlier this year. Emmy Love arrived around the same time that her brother celebrated his first birthday, which places both of their birthdays in May.
