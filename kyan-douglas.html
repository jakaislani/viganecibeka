    Kyan Douglas- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

    Kyan Douglas was born in Miami, Florida on May 5, 1970. He is of Caucasian descent and a United States citizen. His sister, Kelli, raised him in Tampa and Tallahassee and taught him how to manage his hair. His mother’s name is Judith Elaine Douglas. Eddie afterward relocated to Austin to pursue a degree in acting at the University of Texas at Austin. He relocated to New Orleans in his mid-twenties to study theatre at Loyola University, where he got a degree in TV Production.

    Kyan Douglas- Relationship, Married Life

    Kyan Douglas is a gay man who is currently unattached. He was in a relationship with movie and television star Robert Grant. They were together for a short time in 2003, from August to December. They’ve been pals ever since. From March 2004 to August 2006, he was in a committed relationship with publicist and homosexual rights activist Gregory Durham.     Kyan Douglas- Professional Career

    Douglas moved to New York City and started working in sales for Aveda before helping to establish the Aveda Institute, a hairstyling and grooming school. After finishing first in his class, he enrolled in its cosmetology school and worked as a colorist at the New York-based hair salon Arrojo Studio. He began his career by appearing on the reality shows What Not to Wear and While You Were Out. In 2002, an Arrojo customer informed him of a casting call for a makeover show.

    Kyan gave the producers a picture of himself as well as a brief bio he’d written. He was called in for an audition and was soon named the show’s Grooming Guru. Kyan appeared on soundtrack CD and DVD-based events, magazine covers, and discussion shows as a member of Queer Eye. Kyan is also the inventor of Beautified and a L’Oréal product ambassador. In 2004, he was asked to speak at his alma mater, Loyola University New Orleans. In 2009, he took over as host of TLC’s Ten Years Younger (beginning with the fourth season).
