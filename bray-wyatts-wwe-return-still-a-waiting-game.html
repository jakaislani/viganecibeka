    Bray Wyatts WWE Return Still A Waiting Game

    WWE fans are still waiting for Bray Wyatts return to the company, and the company is still going without the Eater of Worlds. Now, with Money in the Bank on the horizon, and SummerSlam to follow, fans are wondering when he might reemerge.

    17 May 2024, 00:00

    WWE fans are still waiting for Bray Wyatt’s return to the company, and the company is still going without the Eater of Worlds. Now, with Money in the Bank on the horizon, and SummerSlam to follow, fans are wondering when he might reemerge.

    Bray Wyatt was back in WWE, and going strong as well. The Eater of Worlds had a match scheduled against Bobby Lashley at WrestleMania, but things ended for that contest when Wyatt vanished on the road to the show of shows.

    Ringside News reached out to inquire once again about Bray Wyatt’s return status. We are told, at this time, there is still no word on his return to the company’s television storylines.

    Bray Wyatt has since been removed from internal rosters. He is also not listed on any touring schedules. At this time, he is not cleared for a return to the ring after suffering from an undisclosed illness on the road to WrestleMania.

    Bray Wyatt was allegedly spotted in Burbank during WrestleMania weekend. You can click here to check out that footage. Wyatt was also spotted over the last week with JoJo Offerman, and he seemed to be in good spirits.

    We will have to keep waiting for Bray Wyatt’s return to the company. Right now, there are no creative ideas being discussed for him, because he is not cleared for a return quite yet.

    On the other hand, Ringside News was able to exclusively report that there is plan for Randy Orton’s return to the company. Only time will tell if Bray Wyatt’s return happens sooner than The Viper.

    That’s your take on this situation with Bray Wyatt’s WWE hiatus? Are you tired of waiting at this point? Sound off in the comments!
