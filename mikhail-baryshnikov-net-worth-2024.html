    Early Life and Career

    Mikhail Baryshnikov was born in Riga, Latvia, and began his ballet studies at the age of 12. His exceptional talent was evident early on, and he quickly rose to prominence in the ballet world. After defecting to Canada in 1974, he joined the American Ballet Theatre (ABT) and later the New York City Ballet, where he worked with legendary choreographers like George Balanchine and Jerome Robbins.

    Acting Career

    In addition to his ballet career, Baryshnikov also ventured into acting. He starred in several films and television shows, most notably the film “White Nights” and the television series “Sex and the City”. His acting roles added a significant amount to his net worth.
