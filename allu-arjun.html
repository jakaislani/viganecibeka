    Biography/Wiki

    Allu Arjun was born on 8 April 1983 (36 years; as in 2018) in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

    He completed his school education at St. Patrick School, Chennai and BBA degree from MSR College, Hyderabad. He is was the most googled Tollywood star in 2016. He is lovingly called Bunny and Stylish Star by his near and dear ones. From the list of 100 celebrities, he was ranked 80th in the Forbes India Celebrity in 2014, 42nd in 2015 and 43rd in 2016 in earnings and 59th in fame.

    Physical Appearance

    Allu Arjun is a good looking and a dashing guy who is approximately 5’9” tall and has a fit body weighs around 70 kg. He has a build with a chest to be around 42 inches and biceps are 15 inches. He has brown eyes, light brown hair and has a decent beard and moustaches.

    Family, Caste & Wife

    Allu Arjun was born into a Hindu-Telugu family of Hyderabad, India. Now, he lives with his family in Hyderabad, Telangana. His father Allu Arvind is a film producer while mother Nirmala is a homemaker.

    He has two brothers. Allu Venkatesh is an elder and Allu Sirish is younger who is an actor.

    On 6 March 2011, he was married to Sneha Reddy and has one son Allu Ayaan who was born in 2014 and one daughter Allu Arha who was born in 2016.

    Career

    He was interested in acting since his early childhood and used to participates in dancing, acting and sports competitions.

    He has always dreamt of seeing himself on the small screen since his childhood. He started his career when he was just 2 years old as a child artist in the film Vijetha (1985).

    He made his lead debut in 2003 from the film Gangotri for which he won 2 awards.

    He won several awards including Nandi Awards, Filmfare Awards South, CineMAA Awards, Filmfare Best Telugu Actor Award etc. He has been honoured with the Pravasi Ratna Puraskaram (PRP) by the popular Malayalam TV channel Asianet and became the first Telugu star of the generation to receive an award for work in the neighbouring film industry.

    Controversy

    In 2013, the rumour was spread about his divorce to Sneha.
