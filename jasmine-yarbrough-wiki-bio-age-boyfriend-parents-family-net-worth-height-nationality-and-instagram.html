    Jasmine Yarbrough Biography – Jasmine Yarbrough Wiki

    Jasmine Yarbrough is Karl Stefanovic’s new girlfriend, former model, co-founder and designer of footwear brand Mara & Mine.

    Jasmine Yarbrough Age

    She was born on January 29, 1984 in Brisbane, Australia.

    Jasmine Yarbrough Parents

    Her father is packaging executive Bob Yarbrough and her mother Cheryl Yarbrough, is a teacher at Brisbane’s elite private girls school St Margaret’s. Cheryl Yarbrough is a member of the city’s Anglican elite. She is a close relative of the former archbishop of Brisbane and Governor-General Peter Hollingworth.

    Jasmine Yarbrough Family

    She has two siblings, a sister Jade and a brother Josh. All the three siblings are in the fashion industry. Jasmine co-founding footwear brand Mara & Mine and Jade the interiors brand, Merci Maison. Josh Yarbrough lives in Los Angeles where he works as a fashion and art designer and dates New Zealand supermodel, Emily May Baker.

    Jasmine and her sister Jade attended Brisbane Girls Grammar School, to which their parents Cheryl and Bob Yarbrough are benefactors.

    Karl Stefanovic and Jasmine Yarbrough

    Karl Stefanovic and Jasmine met at a Sydney boating party months after his split from Thorburn. In February 2018, Stefanovic announced his engagement to Jasmine. Before their meeting, Jasmine dated Wallabies player Drew Mitchell, later a boyfriend of Delta Goodrem.

    Stefanovic was previously married to Cassandra Thorburn. He met Cassandra Thorburn in 1995 at a party. At the time, Thorburn was a country Victorian girl working on local ABC radio. Thorburn and Stefanovic had three children,Jackson Stefanovic, River Stefanovic, and Ava Stefanovic before separating in September 2016 and divorcing in 2017.
